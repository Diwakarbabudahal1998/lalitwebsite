Admission Procedures

All the interested students can collect the application form from the school for Rs. 500/- (Non-refundable). The students should be filled and submitted with all the complete and accurate information as required. In addition, the following documents should be submitted at the time of submission:

• A valid certificate of date of birth.

• Two passport size photos and two stamp size photos of the child.

• Passport size photographs of the child to be affixed on the registration form and interview slip.

• Attested copy of Birth Certificate

• The child to be registered must be accompanied by the PARENTS.

• An interview of the child registered for admission shall be taken, the date of which will be informed at the time of registration.

• Transfer Certificate from the school last attended.

• A copy of the mark list (report card)

• Written test and interview will be taken in all subjects. • Answer questions smartly and intelligently.

****Note: Admission will be strictly on the basis of merit, of written test and interview.****

Entrance Test

A student must pass a written examination for admission. The examination will consist 80 multiple choice questions: and an essay (English, Mathematics and General Knowledge)


Interview

Students who pass the written examination will have to go through academic and professional interview as a final step of admission procedure.
